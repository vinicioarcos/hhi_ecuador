# PRISMA protocol

## Review question

What does the empirical and policy literature say about sectoral diversification, knowledge-based economy transitions, HHI concentration measures and DEA-based efficiency analysis, with direct relevance to the UAE and comparative relevance to Ecuador?

## Inclusion criteria

- Empirical or policy papers on sectoral diversification.
- Studies using HHI, concentration indices, DEA, or related efficiency tools.
- Literature on resource dependence, structural transformation, knowledge economy and diversification policy.
- Studies on UAE, Ecuador, Latin America or comparable economies.

## Exclusion criteria

- Papers unrelated to diversification or efficiency analysis.
- Articles with unverifiable metadata.
- Duplicates or inaccessible records.

## Search blocks

```text
("sectoral diversification" OR "economic diversification")
AND ("United Arab Emirates" OR UAE)
```

```text
("knowledge-based economy" OR "knowledge economy")
AND diversification
AND DEA
```

```text
(Ecuador OR "Latin America")
AND ("sectoral diversification" OR HHI)
```

## Outputs

- Literature matrix
- APA and BibTeX references
- Narrative synthesis for manuscript introduction and discussion

## Reporting note

This is a targeted structured review for the replication paper, not a claim of completed PRISMA counts until actual database exports are archived.
